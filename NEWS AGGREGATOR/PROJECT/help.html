Help Section

Welcome to the help section of our news aggregator. Here you can find answers to common questions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I use the search feature?
    To search for specific news topics, simply enter your query in the search bar located at the top of the page and press Enter or click the Search button.
  • Can I filter news by category?
    Yes, you can filter news articles by category using the navigation menu on the homepage. Simply click on the category you're interested in to view related articles.
  • How often is the news updated?
    Our news aggregator updates its content continuously throughout the day to provide you with the latest news from various sources.
  • Do you offer a mobile app?
    Currently, we don't have a dedicated mobile app, but our website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, so you can access it easily from your smartphone or tablet.
